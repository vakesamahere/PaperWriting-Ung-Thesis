\MakeAbstract{
    考虑旅客选择的服务网络设计联合确定服务计划并通过离散选择模型捕获内生需求，但由此产生的混合整数规划以难以处理著称。本文提出行程聚合方法（\algoNotation），一种精确框架，将该问题求解到可证明的最优性，并以可行现有解与有效下界配对的形式提供任意时间证书。吸引力平衡技术首先通过重新缩放病态的选择模型系数来稳定数值过程。随后我们识别出标准时间聚合通过允许分数资源分配形成无效供给集而导致严重的收入高估。为消除这一虚假收入来源，我们引入一种新颖的行程聚合方法，限制"超级行程"内吸引力的最大求和。上界模块利用聚合频率固定和行程割，而全局收敛由流平衡网络细化策略保证。在真实世界实例上的计算实验表明，\algoNotation 在固定运行时间预算下将此前不可处理的百万变量问题求解到经认证的近最优，凸显了将旅客选择整合到服务网络设计中的价值。
}{服务网络设计，旅客选择，行程聚合，动态离散化，精确优化}

\MakeAbstractEng{
    Service network design with passenger choice jointly determines service plans and captures endogenous demand through discrete choice models, but the resulting mixed-integer programs are notoriously intractable. We propose the Itinerary Aggregation Approach (\algoNotation), an exact framework that solves this problem to provable optimality and provides anytime certificates in the form of feasible incumbents paired with valid lower bounds. An Attraction Balancing technique first stabilizes the numerical process by rescaling the ill-conditioned choice-model coefficients. We then identify that standard temporal aggregation causes severe revenue overestimation by allowing fractional resource allocations to form invalid offer sets. To eliminate this source of fictitious revenue, we introduce a novel Itinerary Aggregation method that bounds the maximum summation of attractions within ``super-itineraries.'' The upper-bound module leverages aggregated frequency fixing and itinerary cuts, while global convergence is guaranteed by a flow-balance network refinement strategy. Computational studies on real-world instances demonstrate that \algoNotation solves previously intractable million-variable problems to certified near-optimality under fixed runtime budgets, underscoring the value of integrating passenger choice into service network design.
}{Service Network Design, Passenger Choice, Itinerary Aggregation, Dynamic Discretization, Exact Optimization}
